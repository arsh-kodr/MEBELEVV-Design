FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What types of furniture do you offer?
+

We offer a wide range of furniture for living rooms, bedrooms, dining areas, and offices — including sofas, beds, chairs, tables, storage units, and more. Our collection blends modern design with lasting comfort.

Do you offer custom-made furniture?
+

Yes, we do! You can customize dimensions, fabrics, materials, and finishes to suit your space and style. Just reach out to our design team for a free consultation.

What is the estimated delivery time?
+

Most of our products are delivered within 7–14 business days. Custom furniture may take 3–4 weeks depending on design complexity.

Is assembly service included?
+

Yes, professional assembly is included with every order at no additional cost. Our team will ensure everything is perfectly set up in your space.

How much does assembly and delivery cost?
